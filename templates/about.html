About Our Email Classification System

Understanding how we protect you from phishing threats

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a reliable and user-friendly tool that helps individuals and organizations identify and protect themselves from phishing emails. We believe in creating a safer digital environment through advanced technology and education.

Built with Flask

Our application is built using Flask, a lightweight WSGI web application framework in Python. It's designed to make getting started quick and easy, with the ability to scale up to complex applications.

How Our System Works

Email Input

Users submit email content for analysis through our secure interface

Processing

Our system extracts and analyzes key features from the email content

Classification

Machine learning algorithms determine if the email is legitimate or phishing

Results

Users receive instant classification results with confidence scores
